About The Well

The Well is the ultimate after-school spot just for High Schoolers. It's got everything you need, like a chill place to read some books, cool games like basketball, volleyball, and ping pong, and even a coffee bar where you can grab energy spritzers, coffee, and other drinks. When it comes to studying, The Well has got your back with a space that's perfect for group projects or solo study sessions, stocked with all the stuff you need to do your best in School. And if you're into sports, you can shoot hoops, play volleyball, or challenge your friends to some ping pong. And although there are games being played, there's still a laid-back caf√© atmosphere where you can take a breather and sip on some tasty drinks. The Well is all about helping you find the right balance between work and play during your High School journey.

Our Story

Originally intended as a venue for special occasions like graduation and birthday parties, The Well underwent a transformative journey in the Spring and Summer of 2023. The space witnessed extensive renovations, with dedicated efforts to craft a unique environment for the community's youth. Throughout the Summer our team worked tirelessly, and by Mid-Fall The Well opened its doors to host various Youth Group and Church Activities. And finally, on Tuesday, November 28th, 2023, the vibrant and revitalized space welcomed High School Students seeking a haven for study, games, and camaraderie, marking the beginning of an exciting chapter in our community's social landscape.

What's Next?

The Well is gearing up to be the go-to spot for High Schoolers, with exciting plans for the future. The focus is on creating a safe and joyful environment, where students can unwind and study. The expansion plans to include a broader menu, offering more delicious options like snacks and drinks to keep everyone energized. Additionally, the game offerings are set to grow, providing an even wider range of recreational activities for students to enjoy. By enhancing both the menu and game options, The Well aims to ensure a vibrant and dynamic space for students, fostering a sense of community and fun for years to come.
